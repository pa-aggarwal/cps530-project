Sugar On Top

We are a vegan sweets bakery located in Toronto that sell donuts, cupcakes, cakes, and many other delicious treats to our customers.

About Us

Our mission is to create as many vegan-friendly options of traditional baked goods and desserts to cater towards a diverse group of customers each with their own health preferences. To fit each customer's needs, we list each recipe's ingredients on our menu.

Many of our restaurant staff come from different levels of experience but Sugar On Top aims to teach young bakers to challenge the ideas of traditional baking.
